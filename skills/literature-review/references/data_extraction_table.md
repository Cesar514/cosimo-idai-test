# Data Extraction Table Template

Use this template to extract consistent fields across studies. Add/remove columns as needed for the review type.

## Recommended Columns

| Study ID | Citation | Year | Study Design | Population/Setting | Sample Size | Intervention/Exposure | Comparator | Outcomes | Effect Size (metric) | CI/SE | Key Findings | Limitations | Funding/COI | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| S1 | Author et al. | 2022 | RCT | Adults with ... | 120 | ... | ... | ... | OR=1.8 | 95% CI | ... | ... | ... | ... |

## Field Guidance

- **Study Design**: RCT, cohort, case-control, cross-sectional, in vitro, in vivo, etc.
- **Population/Setting**: Inclusion criteria, clinical context, geography.
- **Effect Size (metric)**: OR, RR, HR, SMD, MD, etc.
- **CI/SE**: 95% CI or standard error for meta-analysis readiness.
- **Funding/COI**: Report conflicts of interest and funding sources.
